    Robotics and Software Learning Program for Children

    About the ElementoLabs program

    Elemento delivers one box every month, to your doorstep, for 3 months. Each box contains material for several projects. All equipments (including electronic components, sensors, connectors, robocar wheels, chassis, and more), step-by-step instructions booklet and unlimited teacher support over phone are provided. Children learn basic and advance electronics, Robotics and Programming. This also helps them develop interest in Science, Technology, Engineering & Mathematics (STEM).

    Why Robotics & Coding

    Robots and Software are a big part of our future - most jobs will require deep understanding of these.

    In countries like US, Singapore and China, children are already learning these important skillset.

    Teaching Robotics to kids is an excellent way to foster a love for science and engineering that will grow with them as they get older.

    Let your child showcase their talent in community / school by demonstrating the projects.
