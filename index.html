    Your questions answered

    What happens after I purchase elemento?
    You will receive an SMS and email once you place the order. You will receive one box per month for 3 months. Your first box will reach you within 7-10 business days of placing your order. .
    What does each elemento box contain?
    Each Elemento Box contains all necessary parts to build the projects (sensors, wires, LEDs, chassis, wheels etc). Each Box also contains an easy-to-follow instructional booklet that has step-by-step instructions and explanation of each project.
    I'm a busy parent. I don't have enough time. Will my child be able to do the projects independently?
    The Elemento Projects are designed for children, and can be done by them without any supervision. The Instructional booklet is thorough enough for children. Our Teachers are just a call away In case any child needs help.
    How are the Elemento Projects structured?
    Elemento Projects start with basics, and then gradually build upon the concepts. By the end of the program, your child will be proficient in Robotics and Electronics.
    Can I cancel the program at any time?
    If at all you feel that Elemento Labs isn't working out for your child, no worries! Just send us an email and we'll promptly refund the amount for the remaining boxes back to your bank account!
    Can I change my address in the middle
    Absolutely! Just shoot us an email at care@elementolabs.com and we'll promptly ship the box to your new address.
    Is shipping free? To which places do you ship?
    Oh yes! Shipping is absolutely free! You don't have to pay any additional cost and we ship all across the world.
    Is there any soldering required to build Projects?
    No soldering is required to build the Projects.
